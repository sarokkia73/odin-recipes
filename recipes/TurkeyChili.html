Turkey Chili

Description

A hearthy and easy chili with minced turkey and sweet potatoes!

Ready in 30 minutes with minimal prep.

Ingredients

  • Minced turkey
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Onions
  • Chickpeas
  • Passata
  • Paprika
  • Chilli powder

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and the sweet potatoes.
  2. Add oil to the pot; cook the onions until golden, then add the other ingredients.
  3. Close the lid, then cook for 5-8 minutes at high pressure.
  4. Quick release when ready. You can add yogurt or parsley to serve.
