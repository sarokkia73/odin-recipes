Shakshuka

Description

A quick and veggie-loaded breakfast dish.

Few ingredients for a big result.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Peppers, cut into stipes
  • Onions, chopped
  • Chopped tomatoes

Steps

  1. Coook the onions until golden.
  2. Add peppers and chopped tomatoes and cook until softened..
  3. Crack eggs on top, cover and cook for about 5 minutes, until eggs are set.
  4. Plate up and enjoy!
